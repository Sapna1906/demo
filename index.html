1. Added sugar -
. Added sugars are sugars that are added to foods during processing, cooking, or before eating, and include things like table sugar, honey, syrups, and concentrated fruit juices.
. Excessive consumption of added sugars can lead to various health problems, including tooth decay, obesity, type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and high triglyceride levels.
2. Carbonated water -
. Carbonated water can cause bloating because the bubbles get trapped in your gastrointestinal tract.
. If you're prone to bloating, you should avoid carbonated drinks.
. Carbonated water can erode tooth enamel over time, especially if consumed in excess.
